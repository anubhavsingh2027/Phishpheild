🔎 About PhishShield

Protecting users from phishing threats — fast, simple, and free!

🛡️ Our Mission

At PhishShield, our mission is to provide quick and reliable phishing detection tools, empowering users to browse the internet safely and confidently. We believe that cybersecurity should be simple, effective, and accessible to everyone.

🚀 Who We Are

PhishShield was founded by Anubhav Singh, a passionate Computer Science and AI student dedicated to making the internet a safer place. With strong skills in web development, cybersecurity, and AI, Anubhav designed PhishShield to offer smart detection in an easy-to-use way.
